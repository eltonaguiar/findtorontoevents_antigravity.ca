## METHODOLOGY

### How the Confluence Engine Works

1. **12 proven strategies** run independently on each bar
2. Each strategy has a **weight** based on backtest performance (momentum/breakout weighted higher)
3. A **BUY signal fires** when:
   - Weighted confluence score >= 45% of maximum possible
   - At least 3 individual strategies agree
   - RSI(14) < 80 (not overbought)
   - 5-bar cooldown since last signal
   - Risk:Reward >= 1.5:1
4. **Stop Loss** = max(2x ATR below entry, recent support - 0.5%)
   - Capped at 8% max risk per trade
5. **Take Profit** = ATR-based, regime-adjusted:
   - Bull: 4x ATR | Sideways: 3x ATR | Bear: 2x ATR
   - Scaled by confidence score
   - Capped at nearest resistance
6. **Max hold:** 30 days — if neither TP nor SL hit, close at market

### Honest Limitations

- Daily timeframe only — misses intraday moves
- No commission modeled on TP/SL exits (would reduce PnL by ~0.1-0.2% per trade)
- SL assumes execution at exact SL price (slippage could worsen losses)
- Backtested on historical data — future regimes may differ
- Confluence signals are INFREQUENT by design — patience required
- Max drawdown can still be significant during extended bear markets
