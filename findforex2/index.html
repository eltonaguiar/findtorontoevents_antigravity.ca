About the Forex Scanner

The Forex Scanner analyzes 15 major forex pairs using 8 independent trading strategies to identify high-probability trading opportunities.

Each strategy combines multiple technical indicators including momentum, trend analysis, support/resistance levels, and breakout patterns. Signals are generated based on confluence across multiple timeframes and indicators.

All signals are tracked with backtesting capabilities, allowing you to analyze historical performance, compare strategies, and find optimal trading parameters. The scanner provides comprehensive portfolio analysis tools for forex traders.
