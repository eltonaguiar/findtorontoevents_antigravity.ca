Illaoi is a champion in 2XKO. The Truth-Bearer of Nagakabouros, Illaoi wields a massive totem to test the will of her enemies and summon spectral tentacles to fight by her side. She's a high-damage lockdown bruiser who overwhelms enemies with pressure as immense as her faith.[3]

Unique Mechanic - Tentacles: All of Illaoi's special moves create tentacles on hit. these tentacles are used for all of Illaoi's special moves giving them additional hits used for combos and pressure.

Pick if you like
  • Hard-hitting, long reaching attacks
  • High damage and corner carry off of many hits
  • Excellent assists for neutral, pressure, and combos
  • Best-in-class corner pressure and okizeme, able to set up teammate's pressure very well
  • High skill ceiling for optimizing routing and pressure sequences
Avoid if you dislike
  • A two-step gameplan, relying on tentacles on the screen for maximum effectiveness
  • Slow, committal neutral that can struggle against faster characters or zoning
  • No real mixup options of her own, relying on her teammate to open up passive opponents
