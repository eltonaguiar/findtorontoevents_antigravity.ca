Top Places to Visit in Toronto

1. CN Tower

Touch Toronto's skyline at the CN Tower! Soar to breathtaking heights for a birds eye view that paints the city below. Elevate your experience with the Glass Floor and thrilling EdgeWalk. Whether it's stunning vistas, heart-pounding adventures, or unforgettable dining, every moment at the CN Tower is a peak experience!


2. Toronto Island Park

Nature whispers in Toronto Island Park and makes your life stress-free. Dwell into lush greenery and skyline views. Kids would love the Amusement Park meanwhile, adults can watch a peaceful sunset. For the best experience, rent a bike or take a ferry ride!


3. St. Lawrence Market

Take a stroll through St. Lawrence Market's streets on a sunny morning in Toronto. Your Toronto visit will be worthwhile once you shop and eat in this market! Whatever your cravings, the market fulfills your wishes. There is nothing ordinary about the market. It carries centuries-old traditions and unites cultures, people, values, and especially food!


4. Casa Loma

Set on a hill in Toronto overlooking the city. The three levels of the castle are decorated like ancient times at Casa Loma . The Canadian history is finely preserved in the exhibitions for spectators to observe. This unique castle also plays a starring role in movies, making it a real-life Hollywood gem in Toronto! Pro tip: Do not miss the finest beef in the world when you visit the castle!


5. Royal Ontario Museum

Dive into a world of wonder at the Royal Ontario Museum ! Unearth ancient treasures, discover captivating exhibits, and stroll through halls echoing with history. From dinosaurs to cultural artifacts, every visit is a journey through time. Visit the Royal Ontario Museum in Toronto without fail to have a sneak peek at the World's artifacts and culture.


6. Distillery District

Step into Toronto's past at the Distillery District . Cobblestone streets create an enchanting blend of art, culture, and flavor in the district. Historic charm meets modern vibes, offering unique shops, cozy cafes, and art galleries. It's a vibrant tapestry of heritage and creativity, making every visit a delightful journey through time!


7. Steam Whistle Brewery

Satisfy your thirst at Steam Whistle Brewery by sipping the taste of Toronto's brewing legacy. Take a tour of the vibrant brewery, soak in the lively atmosphere, and savor the freshness of crafted beers. It is satisfying to see the process at the brewery. It's a bubbly blend of history, hops, and happiness the ultimate stop for beer enthusiasts!


8. Toronto Zoo

Reserve a day to visit the Toronto Zoo if you are fond of animals. The zoo aims to bring people, animals, and science together to survive the extinction of rare animals. Explore the zoo in Scenic Safari Drive-Thru or enjoy the new one-way walking routes. Explore diverse habitats, catch captivating shows, and create unforgettable family memories.


9. Ripleys Aquarium of Canada

Dive into an underwater wonderland at Ripley's Aquarium of Canada , North Americas longest underwater viewing tunnel. The aquarium consists of 9 galleries with saltwater and freshwater environments. It's an oceanic odyssey where interactive exhibits, vibrant displays, and thrilling shows create a splash of awe. Come for the fish, stay for the adventure right in the heart of Toronto!


10. Art Gallery of Ontario

The art lover inside you will pop out at the Art Gallery of Ontario . With over 90,000 artworks, it's a cultural powerhouse. Marvel at iconic Canadian and Inuit masterpieces, European elegance, and contemporary coolness. After exploring, dine at the award-winning restaurant FRANK. Lastly, please please your eyes with beautiful jewelry, gifts, and beautiful art books!
