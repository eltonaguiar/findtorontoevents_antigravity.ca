CRITICAL FAILURES & ROOT CAUSES

Failure #1: Commission Economics

The Math:

  • 417 trades × $10 commission × 2 (round trip) = $8,340
  • Average win: +0.72% on $1,000 position = $7.20
  • Average loss: -12.01% on $1,000 position = -$120.10
  • Each trade costs $20 in commissions alone

Solution:

  • Switch to zero-commission brokers (Wealthsimple Trade, IBKR Lite)
  • Reduce trade frequency by 80%
  • Increase target profit to 15-20% minimum

Failure #2: Stop Loss Execution

The Problem:

  • Stated stop: -3%
  • Actual average loss: -12.01%
  • Losses are 4x larger than intended

Root Causes:

  1. Slippage blowing through stops
  2. Gap risk (overnight/weekend)
  3. Stop loss not executed properly
  4. Position sizing errors (one trade: -145%)

Solution:

  • Implement ATR-based dynamic stops
  • Reduce position sizes
  • Add guaranteed stop orders where available

Failure #3: Over-Trading

The Problem:

  • 417 trades over ~23 months = ~18 trades/month
  • High frequency = high friction
  • Most trades (67%) hit max hold and expire

Solution:

  • Increase signal threshold (only trade 80+ confidence)
  • Reduce scan frequency
  • Focus on higher timeframes

APPENDIX: KEY METRICS DEFINITIONS

Return Metrics

  • Total Return: (Final Value / Initial Value) - 1
  • Annualized Return: (1 + Total Return)^(365/Days) - 1
  • Alpha: Excess return vs benchmark
  • Information Ratio: Alpha / Tracking Error

Risk Metrics

  • Sharpe Ratio: (Return - Risk Free) / Volatility
  • Sortino Ratio: (Return - Risk Free) / Downside Volatility
  • Calmar Ratio: Annual Return / Max Drawdown
  • Max Drawdown: Peak to trough decline

Trade Metrics

  • Win Rate: Winning Trades / Total Trades
  • Profit Factor: Gross Profit / Gross Loss
  • Expectancy: (Win% × Avg Win) - (Loss% × Avg Loss)
  • Payoff Ratio: Avg Win / Avg Loss

Statistical Significance

  • Deflated Sharpe Ratio: Adjusts for multiple testing
  • T-Statistic: Significance of returns
  • P-Value: Probability results are random
