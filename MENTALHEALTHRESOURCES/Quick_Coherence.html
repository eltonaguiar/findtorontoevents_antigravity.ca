The Science Behind QCT

The Quick Coherence Technique was developed by the HeartMath Institute based on decades of research on heart-brain communication.

Key benefits:

  • Improved HRV: Increases heart rate variability, a key marker of resilience
  • Reduced cortisol: Lowers stress hormones within minutes
  • Enhanced clarity: Improves cognitive function and decision-making
  • Emotional regulation: Helps shift from negative to positive emotional states

When to use: Before stressful situations, during moments of anxiety, to improve focus, or anytime you need emotional reset.
