Benefits of Gratitude Journaling

  • Improved mood: Regularly noting positives shifts focus from negative thoughts
  • Better sleep: Reflecting on gratitude before bed promotes restful sleep
  • Reduced stress: Appreciation helps put challenges in perspective
  • Stronger relationships: Gratitude increases empathy and reduces aggression
  • Increased resilience: Helps you bounce back from difficult experiences
