Mindfulness Tips

  • Find a comfortable position: Sit or lie down in a relaxed posture
  • Focus on your breath: Notice the sensation of breathing without changing it
  • When your mind wanders: Gently return your attention to your breath
  • No judgment: Accept whatever thoughts arise without criticism
  • Practice regularly: Even 5 minutes daily can make a difference
