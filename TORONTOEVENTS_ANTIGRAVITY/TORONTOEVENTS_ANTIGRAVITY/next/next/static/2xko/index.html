Skins & Aesthetic Items

2XKO offers a wide variety of cosmetic items to personalize your experience. Customize your champions, avatars, and more with unique skins, chromas, emotes, and other aesthetic items.

Character Skins

Transform your champions with alternate skins. Most characters have multiple skin options, including:

  • Dynasty Ahri
  • Bladesong Yasuo
  • Arcane skins (Ekko, Vi, Jinx)
  • And many more!

All skins feature customizable color variants (chromas) to match your style.

Other Cosmetics

Beyond character skins, 2XKO offers:

  • Avatar Items: Customize your player avatar
  • Emotes: Express yourself with unique animations
  • Stickers: Personalize your profile
  • Finishers: Epic victory animations
  • Player Cards: Showcase your achievements

How to Get Cosmetics

Store Purchases

Buy cosmetics directly with KO Points (premium currency) from the in-game store.

Battle Pass

Earn cosmetics through both free and premium Battle Pass tracks. Premium track includes full skins and exclusive items.

Free Rewards

Unlock chromas, avatar items, and emotes using Credits earned through gameplay, character mastery, and missions.

Currency System: 2XKO uses three currencies: KO Points (premium, real money), Champion Tokens (unlock characters), and Credits (free, earned through gameplay, 12,000 limit).

About 2XKO

2XKO is a free-to-play 2v2 fighting game developed by Riot Games, featuring characters from the League of Legends universe. The game combines fast-paced action with strategic team play, where two players team up to take on another duo.

Season 1 launched alongside the console release on January 20, 2026, bringing new content including a new champion and Battle Pass. The game initially launched in early access on PC in October 2025.

With full cross-platform play and cross-progression, you can play with friends on any platform and your progress syncs automatically across all devices.
